Akihiro Mugi

As the eldest son from the Mugi family, Akihiro had already trained to be a swordsman since early age as the successor of Akihiro's father. Now Akihiro is considered to be the best samurai in the world of Bakudan.

Eiji Fuka

Eiji refuses to use a sword as his main weapon because of his past when a group of evil samurai tried to end his life. Now he uses a spear and a schimitar as his main weapon after training for 20 years so that he can protect his own.

Kazuo

Kazuo was once the head bodyguard of Mugi family and had been close friends with Akihiro. But then he was framed by his fellow bodyguard and was kicked out of the Mugi Household. After planning for revenge he secretly trained with Akihiro to gain more strength.

Toshio

Toshio is an assassin from another land. His name was feared in the battlefield and people would ignore problems if it is related to Toshio. His swordsmanship is excellent and is on the same level with Akihiro. Toshio travelled to Bakudan for a new life, but is it going to change for him?

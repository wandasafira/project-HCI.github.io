About Game

Kenshin Impact is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by Mihoyoman. Kenshin impact takes place in a fantasy world of Bakudan. The game is about a team of Samurai that is going to prevent the destruction of the world from monsters and aliens. Players can either join forces to fight the enemies or take part in fighting againts the heroes. The game features an open-world environment and action-based battle system using elemental magic and swordmanships. The game contains 4 main elements such as fire, water, ice and wind.
